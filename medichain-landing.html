Our Process

How MediChain Works

Step 01
Connect Your Wallet

Sign in with MetaMask. No passwords, no forms. Your wallet is your identity — cryptographically secure and entirely self-sovereign.

Step 02
Mint Your Records

Each medical document becomes an ERC-721 NFT on Polygon, stored encrypted on IPFS. The hash lives on-chain. Only you hold the decryption key.

Step 03
Grant Doctor Access

Approve access via smart contract with expiry dates. Revoke anytime with a single transaction. No middlemen, no data brokers — ever.
